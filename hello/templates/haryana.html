Overview

Haryana, a state nestled in northern India, offers a captivating tapestry of experiences for travelers. Steeped in rich history and blessed with natural beauty, Haryana promises an unforgettable journey. Here's a glimpse of what awaits you:


Cultural Delights:

Kurukshetra: Embark on a pilgrimage to Kurukshetra, the legendary battlefield of the epic Mahabharata. Explore sacred temples, historical sites, and monuments that commemorate this timeless tale.
Panipat: Dive into Haryana's vibrant past at Panipat, renowned for its historical battles and textile industry. Visit ancient Panipat Fort and museums showcasing the region's cultural heritage.
Rohtak: Explore Rohtak, a city known for its wrestling traditions and archaeological marvels. Witness the iconic Rohtak Fort and unwind in the serene ambience of Rohtak Lake.
Surajkund Mela: Immerse yourself in the vibrant Surajkund Mela, a colorful crafts fair showcasing India's rich heritage. Witness traditional performances, shop for handcrafted souvenirs, and savor delectable cuisines from across the country.


Natural Splendor:

Aravalli Hills: Seek serenity amidst the majestic Aravalli Hills, a mountain range offering breathtaking views and a haven for nature enthusiasts. Explore hiking trails, unwind in resorts, or simply soak in the beauty of the landscape.
Wildlife Sanctuaries: Haryana boasts several wildlife sanctuaries, including the renowned Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary and the Bir Shikhar Wildlife Sanctuary. Spot a variety of bird species and animals, making it a paradise for nature lovers and wildlife photographers.
Beautiful Lakes: Haryana is dotted with picturesque lakes offering tranquility and recreational activities. Take a boat ride on the tranquil Karnal Lake or enjoy a picnic amidst the scenic ambience of Badkhal Lake.


Adventurous Activities:

Adventure Sports: Haryana caters to adventure enthusiasts with opportunities for activities like hot air ballooning, paragliding, and rock climbing.
Golfing Paradise: Tee off at world-class golf courses nestled amidst scenic landscapes, offering an unparalleled golfing experience.

How To Reach




Train:

Haryana has a well-connected railway network, with several trains running to major cities within the state and connecting them to other parts of India.
Major railway junctions in Haryana include:
Ambala Cantonment Junction (UMB)
Bhiwani Junction (BWN)
Faridabad Junction (FDB)
Gurgaon Junction (Ggn)
Hisar Junction (HSR)
Karnal Junction (KLN)
Panipat Junction (PNP)
Rohtak Junction (ROH)

Bus:

Haryana has a good network of bus routes connecting its cities and towns. Major bus terminals in Haryana include:
Inter State Bus Terminus (ISBT) in Chandigarh
ISBT in करनाल (Karnal)
ISBT in Hisar
ISBT in Rohtak
Both government and private bus companies operate services within Haryana. Government buses are generally cheaper, while private buses may offer more amenities. Travelers can inquire about schedules and fares at the respective bus terminals or online through the websites of the operating companies.

Air:

While Haryana doesn't have its own domestic airport, travelers can fly into the nearby Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL) in Delhi.
Delhi is well-connected to all major Indian cities, offering easy access to Haryana via onward travel options. From Delhi, taxis, cabs, or car rentals can be used to reach various destinations within Haryana.
